# Demo Script

This script is designed for a 3-5 minute Band of Agents hackathon walkthrough.

## 1. Open the Live Demo

Open:

```text
https://quasar.quasarlabs.in
```

Introduce Quasar:

> Quasar is an advisory-only enterprise market intelligence and governance system. It does not place trades, does not generate execution instructions, and does not produce buy/sell signals.

## 2. Show Live Intelligence

Show the MCX NATURALGAS and Forex XAUUSD panels.

Point out:

- Plotly candlestick charts.
- Latest candle summary.
- Market structure labels.
- Timeframe selector.

Explain:

> The chart timeframe controls visual context only. Specialist review uses a multi-timeframe intelligence snapshot, not a single chart timeframe.

## 3. Run Agent Swarm Review

Scroll to Agent Swarm Review and click:

```text
Get Specialist Analysis
```

Explain:

> The charts show market data. Agent Swarm Review transforms that data into specialist-reviewed advisory intelligence.

During execution, point out that the summary remains pending until the final review completes.

## 4. Explain Specialist Cards

After completion, walk through the specialist cards:

- Requirement: what question is being reviewed.
- Market Intelligence: dominant and alternative market thesis.
- System Readiness: freshness, session state, and evidence quality.
- Risk Governance: confidence, conflict, and validation risk.
- Delivery Planning: validation focus.
- Final Review: executive advisory assessment.

Explain:

> Each Band specialist reviews Quasar intelligence artifacts such as regime, scenario, hierarchy, market memory, persistence, validation, and governance evidence.

## 5. Explain Final Advisory Assessment

Show the executive summary strip:

- Decision State
- Market Regime
- Structure Confidence
- Conflict Level
- Dominant Hypothesis

Then show the detailed advisory fields:

- Executive Summary
- Dominant Hypothesis
- Alternative Hypothesis
- Why Not Confirmed
- Key Evidence
- Next Validation
- Safety Mode

Reinforce:

> This section is a reviewed advisory conclusion, not an execution instruction.

## 6. Show Confidence Attribution

Open the Confidence Attribution section.

Explain:

> Confidence is derived deterministically from existing engine outputs such as hierarchy alignment, scenario dominance, structure persistence, multi-timeframe agreement, and conflict score. No random weighting or hidden ML model is introduced.

## 7. Show Governance and Audit Console

Navigate to:

```text
Logs & Review Notes
```

Show the renamed console:

```text
System Audit & Governance Console
```

Highlight:

- Deployment Readiness
- Readiness score: 100
- Readiness state: READY
- Safety status: ADVISORY_ONLY
- Agent Workflow Timeline
- Governance Evidence Summary
- Decision Audit Trail
- Raw System Logs

## 8. Close with Safety and Fit

Conclude:

> Quasar demonstrates how Band agents can support regulated and high-stakes workflows by converting live market intelligence into specialist-reviewed, evidence-linked, auditable advisory assessments while preserving an advisory-only safety boundary.
